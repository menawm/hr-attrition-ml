# HR Attrition Prediction & Retention Strategy

## Overview

This project uses machine learning to predict employee attrition and quantify the financial impact of targeted retention strategies.

The goal is to move from reactive HR decision-making to a proactive, data-driven approach that reduces cost and improves workforce stability.

---

## Key Results

- **$72M** estimated annual attrition cost without intervention  
- Predictive model identifies high-risk employees with high accuracy  
- **$8M–$13M** in potential annual savings through targeted retention  
- **300%+ ROI** on retention strategies  

---

## Why This Matters

Attrition is often treated as unavoidable. This project shows it is **predictable, measurable, and financially controllable**.

Targeted interventions outperform broad retention efforts and deliver meaningful business impact.
